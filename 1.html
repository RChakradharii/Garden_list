Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh:

Part of the famed Mughal Gardens, these two parks display exquisite Persian architecture and are located on the banks of Dal Lake, offering stunning views of the Himalayas.

Location: City - kashmir,Srinagar, Country - India

Hanging Gardens:

These terraced gardens offer panoramic views of the Mumbai skyline and the Arabian Sea. They are a popular spot for picnics, joggers, and families.

Location: City - maharashtra,Mumbai,Country - India

Lalbagh Botanical Garden:

One of the largest botanical gardens in Asia, Lalbagh is home to a diverse collection of plants from around the world. It also has a glasshouse, a bonsai collection, and a lake

Location: City - Karnataka,Bangluru,Country - India

Rock garden:

This unique garden, created by Nek Chand Saini using waste materials, is a must-see for art lovers. It features sculptures, waterfalls, and mosaics made from recycled objects.

Location: City - Chandigarh, Country - India

Indira Gandhi Tulip Garden:

This garden is the largest tulip garden in Asia, with over 60 varieties of tulips on display. It is open for a short time each year in the spring.

Location: City - Srinagar, Country - India
